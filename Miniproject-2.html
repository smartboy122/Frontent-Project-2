Human Care Experts

The seeds of our origin were sown as early as 1953 when the founder of the Manipal Education and Medical Group (MEMG), Dr. T.M.A. Pai, established the Kasturba Medical College in Manipal, Karnataka. Manipal Hospitals as an entity came into existence in 1991 with the launch of our 650-bed flagship hospital at Old Airport Road, Bangalore. Today, we are one of India’s leading healthcare groups with over 10500 beds across 37 hospitals.

Our core values are built around the thought of patient-first and that each doctor at Manipal Hospitals is a human care expert, going above and beyond the call of duty as they live by the belief that every single life is priceless. Apollo Hospitals Bangalore - an Overview

Welcome to Apollo Hospitals Bangalore, the gold standard of quality and compassionate medical treatment located right in the centre of the city.

Commitment to Excellence

As the best multispeciality hospital in Bangalore, we stand tall in our commitment to delivering world-class medical care and innovative treatments, setting benchmarks in the healthcare industry. At Apollo Hospitals, we take great satisfaction in being a pillar of expertise, dependability, and trust. With decades of experience, we have built a reputation for offering the best healthcare services possible, making us the first choice for anyone seeking excellent medical attention in Karnataka.

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure and Technology

Our hospital exemplifies cutting-edge technology, a wide range of medical specialisations, and a dedicated staff of healthcare experts. As the best hospital in Bangalore, our commitment to excellence is evident in our state-of-the-art infrastructure equipped with the latest advancements in medical technology. From diagnostics to treatment, our facilities are designed to meet international standards, ensuring precise and efficient care across various medical disciplines.
